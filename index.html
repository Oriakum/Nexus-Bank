Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Banking App Secure?

Yes, our app uses advanced encryption technology, secure login methods like biometrics, and regular updates to protect your personal information.

How Do I Reset My Password?

Open the app and select "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Follow the prompts to verify your info.

Can I Pay Bills Through The App?

Yes, you can use the "Bill Pay" option. Add biller information, enter amount, and confirm payment.

What Are The Daily Transfer Or Withdrawal Limits?

It depends on your account type. You can check limits in the “Account Settings”.

How Can I View My Transaction History?

Go to “Transactions” or “Account History” in the app to see all your recent transactions.

What Should I Do If I See An Unauthorized Transaction?

Use the “Report Fraud” feature in the app or contact customer care immediately.
